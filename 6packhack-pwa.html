The Golden Rules

Master these 5 fundamentals and you can walk into any restaurant in America and stay on mission. This is the entire system in one page.

1
Protein Is Your #1 Priority
The building block of muscle ‚Äî non-negotiable. It keeps you full, boosts your metabolism, and signals your body to repair and grow. Every meal starts with one question: "Where is the protein?"
2
Carbs & Sugar Are the Enemy
Bread, buns, wraps, rice, potatoes, fries, ketchup, BBQ sauce, sweet dressings ‚Äî they spike insulin, the fat-storage hormone. Eliminate them and your body is forced to burn stored fat for fuel instead.
3
ü•ë Load Up on Healthy Fats
Fat does NOT make you fat ‚Äî sugar does. Mayo, avocado, ranch, cheese, butter, olive oil ‚Äî these are your allies. They satisfy hunger for hours, support testosterone, and keep you in fat-burning mode. Always say YES to guac, mayo, and avocado.
4
Pile on the Veggies & Flavor
Load every meal with non-starchy vegetables ‚Äî lettuce, tomato, onion, jalape√±os, pickles, spinach. Use ranch, hot sauce, mustard, mayo for flavor. Avoid anything sweet. Veggies + healthy fat dressings = full, satisfied, on-mission.
5
Master the Modification
"No bun." "Add avocado." "Extra mayo." "Ranch on the side." "No ketchup ‚Äî mustard instead." These are your weapons. You are always in control. Ask for it exactly how you want it ‚Äî they will do it.
6
Never Drink Your Carbs
Water, black coffee, unsweetened iced tea ‚Äî that's it. A large Coke is 65g of pure sugar. A "healthy" orange juice is 45g. One bad drink undoes a perfect meal. Diet sodas are acceptable in a pinch. Never drink your calories.

Drink Menu

Alcohol is a toxin your body prioritizes over burning fat. Heavy drinking will sabotage progress. But a well-planned social life is part of the mission. When you drink, you drink with strategy. All beer, sugary cocktails, and regular mixers are officially off the menu.

The Classic Soda-Water Highball
Your Order: "[Spirit] with soda water and a lime." Best: Tito's Vodka, Tanqueray Gin, Bourbon, Don Julio Blanco, Bacardi Superior Rum.
Clear spirits have zero carbs. Soda water is just bubbly water. Impossible to mess up.
The "007" Americano
Your Order: "I'd like a Gin and Soda with a lemon twist, served in a rocks glass."
Tanqueray or Hendrick's adds botanical complexity. Crisp, aromatic, distinctly adult. Zero carbs.
Bourbon & Bubbles
Your Order: "Double Maker's Mark on the rocks, soda water on the side."
Full bourbon flavor. You control the dilution. Encourages sipping, not slamming.
The Dry Martini
Your Order: "A Tito's Martini, extra dry, with a twist."
Extra dry = very little vermouth. Almost pure chilled vodka. Clean, potent, undeniably stylish.

Crush Your Cravings: Dessert Protocol

A craving doesn't mean you're weak ‚Äî it means you're human. A true Alpha doesn't let cravings derail the mission. You outsmart them.

THE RULES: No flour. No sugar. No crusts. No ice cream.

The Classic: Berries & Heavy Cream
Ask for mixed berries ‚Äî strawberries, raspberries, blackberries. Specify: "No sugar dusting, no sweet syrup." Ask for a side of real, unsweetened heavy whipped cream (not the aerosol kind). Sweetness hit + quality fats = satisfied and still on mission.
The Power Move: The Cheese Plate
Order the cheese platter at nicer spots. Hard and soft cheeses ‚Äî brie, cheddar, goat cheese. If it comes with almonds or walnuts, you're in business. Completely ignore the crackers, bread, honey, and sugary fruit pastes. High-fat, high-protein finisher that feels decadent.
The Simple Finisher: A Perfect Sip
Sometimes the best dessert isn't a food. A bold black decaf coffee or straight espresso signals the end of the meal. Want it more dessert-like? Ask for a splash of heavy cream (not milk) ‚Äî rich, satisfying, crushes lingering cravings with fat, not sugar.
